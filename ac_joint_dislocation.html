AC Joint Dislocation

Classification according to Rockwood, modification of RW III with AC/DC, GC/PC

Diagnosis: X-ray panorama with 5kg weight-bearing

Treatment:

  • Rockwood I / II: Conservative, use sling for pain relief, passive/active mobilization up to 90Â° abduction/anteversion
  • Rockwood III:
    • Horizontally stable (IIIA): Conservative
    • Unstable (IIIB): Operative if coracoclavicular distance is more than 7mm difference from the opposite side in Alexander View (AJSM 2021, Murphy et al)
  • Rockwood IV-VI: Alexander view bilaterally (for study) → Operative: Bipodal
